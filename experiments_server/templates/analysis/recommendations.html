Methodology

These recommendations are generated by analyzing experimental data from the 4 major experiment series:

  • force_no_force - Compression and recovery behavior under cyclic loading
  • number_of_strands - Effect of strand count on structural properties
  • number_of_layers - Effect of layer count on structural properties
  • strand_thickness - Effect of material thickness on structural properties

Metrics used for each recommendation:

  • Flexibility: Maximum compression percentage achieved from force/no-force experiments
  • Elastic Recovery: Recovery percentage normalized by applied force (% per Newton)
  • Load-Bearing: Maximum force sustained at 10% height reduction
  • Efficiency: Specific load capacity (force / weight × g)

Trade-off averages are calculated using only data from these 4 major experiment series. Rankings are determined by sorting configurations by their respective metrics. "Optimal" parameters represent the most common values among top performers.
